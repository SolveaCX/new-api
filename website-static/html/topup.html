Pricing FAQ

How does the top-up bonus work?

Every top-up earns bonus credit, forever: +$3 on $10, +$8 on $20, +$100 on $200. The bonus lands in your balance instantly and is spent like normal credit. Models are also priced at 60–90% of the official list — stacked with the bonus, as low as 50% of the official price.

Is this a monthly subscription?

No. The self-serve plans are prepaid top-ups. Balance is consumed only when API requests use models.

Which models can use the same balance?

One balance can route across GPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, image, audio and video models through one OpenAI-compatible gateway.

Can I see how the balance is consumed?

Yes. Usage is metered by model, token type and request logs, so teams can review spend and control cost.

When should I choose Enterprise?

Choose Enterprise for larger monthly usage, invoicing, procurement, custom routing discounts, or team-level controls.
