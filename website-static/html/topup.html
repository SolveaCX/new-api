Pricing FAQ

What does a plan include?

A monthly model-usage allowance — up to $45 on Go, $90 on Pro, $300 on Max — spendable across all 328+ models, plus media credits for image and video generation. Short 5-hour and 7-day windows keep the service fast for everyone.

Is this a monthly subscription?

Yes. Plans renew monthly and you can cancel anytime. If you use up the monthly allowance, you can keep going on a pay-as-you-go balance with the same key.

Which models does a plan cover?

Every plan covers GPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek and the image, audio and video models — all through one OpenAI-compatible gateway.

Can I see how the balance is consumed?

Yes. Usage is metered by model, token type and request logs, so teams can review spend and control cost.

When should I choose Enterprise?

Choose Enterprise for larger monthly usage, invoicing, procurement, custom routing discounts, or team-level controls.
