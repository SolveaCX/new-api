OUR MISSION

Remove the distance between a person and a model.

The best AI is advancing every week, but using it still means navigating vendor accounts, incompatible APIs, regional restrictions, billing systems, and infrastructure decisions.

That complexity belongs to us—not to the people trying to build. flatkey puts leading official models behind one key, one balance, and one familiar interface.

Our goal is simple: let every person spend less time accessing AI and more time doing something meaningful with it.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

The product should carry the complexity.

01 · SIMPLE BY DEFAULT

Power without the maze

People should not need to become infrastructure experts before they can use a great model. We make the default path obvious and keep the hard parts behind the interface.

02 · ACCESS OVER GATEKEEPING

The frontier should be reachable

Region, billing method, vendor SDK, or company size should not decide who gets to build with the best AI. One key should be enough to begin.

03 · TRUST MUST BE EARNED

Proof, not promises

Clear prices, real endpoints, public availability targets, security controls, and a human when something goes wrong. Simplicity only works when it is trustworthy.
