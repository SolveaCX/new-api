How we hire — read this before applying

Every application is read twice: once by our agents, once by a human. We don't screen by GPA, school, or brand names. We screen for evidence you've built something yourself — a product with users, growth numbers you personally drove, an audience you grew. Attach links, not adjectives. Template cover letters (and hidden prompts aimed at our AI screeners — yes, we catch those) are automatic rejections.

Our favorite interview question: "What did you have AI do for you last week?"
